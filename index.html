About Jhon Mayday

Jhon Mayday is an up and coming Peruvian artist who aims to one day be recognized for his creative innovations and touch many people's hearts with his music. His music is defined by a mix of rap, reggaeton, and 80's style sounds. His catchphrase, “Real Risk Real Gain”, defines who he is and speaks to the risk-takers of the world. To him, it defines his immigrant standpoint, status, and past experiences he's been through.
